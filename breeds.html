Several Popular Guinea Pig Breeds

Abyssinian Guinea Pig

Image by Tavu - Own work, CC BY-SA 3.0, WikiCommons

One popular breed of guinea pig is the Abyssinian with the ever present messy bed head caused by "rosettes." These little cuties are usually born with between 8-10 rosettes and come in a variety of colors.

Another popular breed of guinea pigs is the Peruvians. These will take a little more care as you have to keep thier hair groomed. It will continue to grow for the life of the cavy. These also come in a variety of different colors and patterns. Fun fact, if you go to a state fair you will often see their hair in curlers or rolled up to keep the cavy from nibbling on it.

American Guinea Pig

Image by minka2507 from Pixabay

The American is the most popular breed of cavies. It is the most iconic with its sleek short hair and many different colors and patterns. You will know this is an American if they don't have any rosettes or long hair on thier bodies. Can you find any other examples of an American cavy on the site?
