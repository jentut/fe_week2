CAVIES Also Known
As GUINEA PIGS

Image by Petra from Pixabay

Guinea pigs, also known as a CAVY or CAVIES, can make a great first pet.

They are easy to care for if you do a little research on what makes them happy. They get along well with other animals, if the other animals are okay with it. They are very playful and make the most adorable happy sounds.
