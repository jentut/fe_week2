Proper Housing for Your Guinea Pig


Here is a great video about the many different types of type of homes for your new friend.

There are a few things you will want to look for in a cage. Luckily, guinea pigs can't jump over things very easily so often cages look short and don't have a top. If you have other animals you will want to get something that will protect them however, if this is your only pet, you don't have to have a full enclosure.

If you are going to house them inside, you will need to make sure you are cleaning the cage often so your room doesn't start to smell like a barn so you will need to find something that is easy to clean.

Another thing to look for is something with high enough sides to keep any bedding or hay contained in their cage. They get the zoomies every once in a while and kick up bedding and hay.

Lastly, make sure there are lots of things for your guinea pig to chew on. Their teeth continually grow so they chew on things to keep them at a good length.
